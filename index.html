Formula: Great Circle Bearing
ΔL = lng(Mecca) − lng(you)
Y = sin(ΔL) × cos(φ_Mecca)
X = cos(φ_you)·sin(φ_Mecca) − sin(φ_you)·cos(φ_Mecca)·cos(ΔL)
Azimuth = atan2(Y, X) mod 360°
